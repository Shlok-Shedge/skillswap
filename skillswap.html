About Skill Swap

SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform built for learners, dreamers, and creators. It connects people who want to share their talents with those eager to learn something new. Instead of spending money on classes, users exchange their skills directly — teaching one another in a fun, supportive, and collaborative environment.

The goal is to create a community-driven learning space where everyone has something to teach and something to learn. Whether it’s coding, music, art, languages, or fitness — SkillSwap makes it easy to connect and grow together.

More about Skill Swap

At SkillSwap, we believe that knowledge should never be limited by cost or access. Every user brings their own unique skill set, and by trading skills instead of money, you become both a teacher and a student at the same time.

Our platform also encourages collaboration, networking, and mentorship — helping you make real connections with people who share your interests and goals.

Developed by: Shlok Shedge, Mani Reddy, and Dhrumil Rughani.

Our Services
